About Teddy Bears

Teddy bears are soft toys in the form of a bear. They are named after Theodore "Teddy" Roosevelt, the 26th president of the United States, and are an iconic children's toy, celebrated in story, song, and film.

Teddy bears are often used to comfort children and are sometimes given to adults to signify love, congratulations, or sympathy.
